Our Doctors

  • Dr. Ray Meas

    Dr Ray Meas is a Cape Town based general practitioner registered with the HPCSA having graduated from the University of Stellenbosch (MBChB) in 2016. He’s worked in Gauteng Province at Helen Joseph Hospital in Johannesburg and managed the emergency department at Mahikeng Provincial Hospital in the Northwest Province. Since moving back to Cape Town he’s had the privilege of managing a GP practice for four years working with families on the Cape Flats in focusing on acute and chronic medical management, child health, reproductive and sexual health, women's health, LGBTQ health, mental health and the Covid-19 pandemic.

    Dr Ray Meas started My Doctor Family Practice as a way to provide healthcare to patients all over the country through the use of the technological services at our disposal. By starting My doctor Family Practice he aims be able to invest in his passion of healing and counselling through call-out services in Cape Town and virtual consultations to the whole of South Africa.

    He believes in affordable, holistic healthcare through treatment, counselling and education to ensure the best quality of life before, during and after illness.
