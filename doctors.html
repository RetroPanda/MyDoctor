Our Doctors

  • Dr. Ray Meas – Founder, My Doctor Family Practice

    Dr. Ray Meas is a queer, Cape Town–based general practitioner registered with the HPCSA. He earned his MBChB from the University of Stellenbosch in 2016 and has gained extensive clinical experience across South Africa, including at Helen Joseph Hospital in Johannesburg and as Emergency Department Manager at Mahikeng Provincial Hospital in the Northwest Province.

    Since returning to Cape Town in 2019, Dr. Meas has dedicated his practice to supporting families on the Cape Flats, focusing on acute and chronic medical care, child health, reproductive and sexual health, women’s health, LGBTQIA+ healthcare, mental health, and pandemic response.

    In 2023, he founded My Doctor Family Practice with the vision of offering compassionate, affordable healthcare that meets the needs often overlooked in the private sector. Through the practice, he provides in-person and home-visit consultations in Cape Town, as well as virtual consultations for clients across South Africa.

    Dr. Meas is certified by the South African HIV Clinicians Society to provide gender-affirming healthcare and welcomes the opportunity to care for transgender and gender-diverse clients. He is committed to holistic, inclusive care for people of all ages, races, genders, sexual orientations, and faiths, combining treatment, counselling, and education to support the best possible quality of life before, during, and after illness.
