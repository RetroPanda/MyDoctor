Our Doctors

  • Dr. Ray Meas

    Dr Ray Meas is a Cape Town based general practitioner registered with the HPCSA having graduated from the University of Stellenbosch (MBChB) in 2016. He’s worked in Gauteng Province at Helen Joseph Hospital in Johannesburg and managed the emergency department at Mahikeng Provincial Hospital in the Northwest Province. Since moving back to Cape Town he’s had the privilege of serving the community of Strandfontein for four years as well as working with families across the Cape Flats in focusing on acute and chronic medical management, child health, reproductive and sexual health, women's health, LGBTQ health, mental health and the Covid-19 pandemic.

    Dr Ray Meas started My Doctor Family Practice in 2023 as a way to provide quality healthcare to patients all over the country through on-line medical consultations. In 2024 he expanded the practice to physical rooms in Strandfontein to include in-person consultations and referrals to on-site physiotherapy and dental services.

    He believes in affordable, holistic healthcare through treatment, counselling and education to ensure the best quality of life before, during and after illness.



  • Dr. Hayley James - Dentist

    Dr. Hayley James leads an on-site dental practice, offering comprehensive dental services to keep your smile healthy and bright. From routine cleanings to advanced dental procedures, Dr. James and her team are here to ensure your oral health is in top condition.



  • Tamzin Vermeulen - Physiotherapist

    Tamzin Vermeulen provides on-site expert physiotherapy services to help you recover from injuries, manage chronic pain, and improve your overall physical health. Her personalized treatment plans are designed to meet your specific needs and promote optimal recovery and well-being.
