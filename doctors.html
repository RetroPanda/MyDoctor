Our Doctors

  • Dr. Ray Meas

    Dr Ray Meas is a queer, Cape Town based general practitioner registered with the HPCSA, having graduated from the University of Stellenbosch (MBChB) in 2016.

    He’s worked in Gauteng Province at Helen Joseph Hospital in Johannesburg and managed the emergency department at Mahikeng Provincial Hospital in the Northwest Province. Since moving back to Cape Town in 2019, he has enjoyed the privilege of working with families on the Cape Flats in focusing on acute and chronic medical management, child healthcare, LGBTQIA+ , reproductive and sexual healthcare, women's health, mental healthcare and the Covid-19 pandemic.

    Dr. Ray Meas founded My Doctor Family Practice in 2023 to provide affordable and compassionate healthcare which he found lacking in the private medical sector. Through My Doctor Family Practice, Dr. Ray Meas invests in his passion for healing and counseling through in-person and home-visit consultations to residents of Cape Town and virtual consultations to clients across South Africa.

    Dr Ray Meas is certified to provide Gender-affirming healthcare by the South African HIV Clinicians Society and welcomes the opportunity to provide compassionate medical care to Transgender and gender diverse clients.

    He believes in affordable, holistic healthcare for every person of every age, race, gender, sexual orientation and faith through treatment, counselling and education to ensure the best quality of life before, during and after illness.
