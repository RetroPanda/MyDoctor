Payment & Billing

We offer simple and flexible payment methods for your convenience:

  • Medical aid claims (practice submits or invoices provided for patient submission, depending on scheme rules)
  • Online booking & payment to confirm appointments
  • In-person payments accepted via Yoco card machine
  • Home visit consultations: EFT payment required prior to consultation

Please note: Medical aid reimbursement is subject to individual scheme benefits and tariffs. Any shortfall remains the patient’s responsibility.
