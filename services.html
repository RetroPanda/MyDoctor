Our Services:

We offer a range of medical services, all delivered by our team of experienced and dedicated healthcare professionals. Some of the services we provide include:

In-Person Consultations

Visit our welcoming practice in Strandfontein for thorough and professional medical consultations. Our experienced team is here to address your health concerns, offering services from routine check-ups to specialized care.


Vitual Consultaions

Enjoy the convenience of receiving medical care from the comfort of your home. Our virtual consultations are designed to provide you with the same level of attention and care as an in-person visit, making healthcare more accessible and flexible.


Call-Out Service*

For patients who cannot visit our practice, we offer call-out services. Our medical team will come to your location, ensuring you receive the necessary care without the need to travel, perfect for homebound individuals or those requiring privacy.


Chronic Disease Management

We specialize in providing comprehensive care for patients with chronic conditions such as diabetes, high blood pressure, asthma, and more. Our doctors will work closely with you to develop personalized treatment plans and monitor your health regularly.

Medication Management

Managing multiple medications can be challenging, especially for older adults. Our doctors will review your medication regimen, provide education on proper usage, and help you establish a routine that ensures you're taking the correct medications at the right time

Health Education

We believe in empowering our patients with knowledge to make informed decisions about their health. Our doctors will provide guidance on various health topics, including diet, exercise, preventive care, and managing specific health conditions.

Referrals

Our access to on-site Dental and Physiotherapy services makes referrals more convenient for you and your loved ones when addressing your healthcare needs.



We provide services to:

  1. Working professionals
  2. Stay-at-home parents
  3. The elderly or chronic illness sufferers who are too ill to be transported
  4. Local and international travellers
  5. LGBTQ individuals
