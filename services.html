Services

Virtual Consultations:

Our virtual consultations offer a convenient and efficient way to access medical advice from the comfort of your own home or office. Through secure video conferencing technology, you can discuss your symptoms, receive a diagnosis, and even get prescriptions, all without leaving your location. Our qualified doctors are available during extended hours, ensuring that you can access medical care when you need it most.

Call-Out Service:

We understand that there are instances where a physical examination is necessary to accurately diagnose and treat certain conditions. That's why we offer a call-out service bringing medical expertise right to your doorstep. Whether you have mobility issues, are bedridden, or simply prefer them. My Doctor Family Practice provides Call-Out visits to anywhere in Cape town, be it your home, office, AirBnB or Hotel.

We provide services to:

  1. Working professionals
  2. Stay-at-home parents
  3. The elderly or chronic illness sufferers who are too ill to be transported
  4. Local and international travellers
  5. LGBTQ individuals who would like to avoid discrimination or distress visiting a GP practice
  6. Anyone who would like to stay clear of overcrowded waiting rooms
  7. Those unable to get an appointment with their usual GP

Our Services: [for each point a picture link]

We offer a range of medical services, all delivered by our team of experienced and dedicated healthcare professionals. Some of the services we provide include:

  1. Vitual Consultaiions Our primary service provides you with a consultation with one of our passionate medical doctors through the use of your mobile smartphone, laptop or computer through a stable internet connection anywhere in South Africa.
  2. Call-Out Service: Upon reviewing the need determined during our telephonic call, this service involves sending our qualified doctors directly to your home or office for medical consultations. Whether you require a routine check-up, treatment for a minor illness, or ongoing medical care, our doctors are here for you.
  3. Chronic Disease Management: We specialize in providing comprehensive care for patients with chronic conditions such as diabetes, high blood pressure, asthma, and more. Our doctors will work closely with you to develop personalized treatment plans and monitor your health regularly.
  4. Medication Management: Managing multiple medications can be challenging, especially for older adults. Our doctors will review your medication regimen, provide education on proper usage, and help you establish a routine that ensures you're taking the correct medications at the right time.
  5. Health Education: We believe in empowering our patients with knowledge to make informed decisions about their health. Our doctors will provide guidance on various health topics, including diet, exercise, preventive care, and managing specific health conditions.
  6. Medical Reports: We offer Fit for work assessments as well as Disability assessments that can be arranged via our Call-Out-Service
  7. Mobile COVID-19 testing: Rapid Antigen Test, PCR Test, Travel PCR Test * All PCR tests include laboratory fees
  8. Corporate / staff wellness and COVID-19 screening
  9. Intravenous Therapy: IV rehydration Fluids (Drip), IV Vitamin Therapy
