About Us

Our mission is to ensure that every patient has access to affordable, compassionate and convenient healthcare when they need it most. We are committed to providing personalized care to each of our patients through in-person visits, virtual consultations, or convenient call-out services.

With a range of medical, dental, and physiotherapy services under one roof, we provide a holistic approach to health and wellness.

- Community Commitment:
We are deeply rooted in the Strandfontein community, treating every patient with the care and respect they deserve.

We look forward to welcoming you to My Doctor Family Practice, where your health and well-being are our top priorities.
