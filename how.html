How it Works

Booking a visit from My Doctor Family Practice is a simple and hassle-free process.


  1. Conveniently book your consultation online :
    Click the “BOOK NOW” icon on our website to select a service and any available timeslot for your appointment. Alternativley contact us through whatsapp or email and we’ll do the rest.
  2. Private rates
    All fees for service can be found on our booking page. Select the “BOOK NOW” icon for a list of our services and up to date fees.
  3. Medical aid claims
    Select any of our services marked “Medical Aid Claim” and book your consultation by providing your medical aid plan, option, dependant code and medical aid number in the spaces provided.
  4. Appointment Confirmation:
    The date and time of your consultation will be confirmed through SMS and/or email to the contact details you’ve provided. We strive to offer flexible scheduling options to accommodate your needs.
  5. Call-Out Service:
    Select the call-out service and choose your date and timeslot. Upon receiving payment, your booking will be arranged within 24 hours. On the scheduled day, our doctor will arrive at your location equipped with the necessary medical equipment and supplies. Our doctors take the time to listen to your concerns, perform necessary examinations, and provide appropriate treatment or guidance.
  6. Follow-up:
    Depending on your medical condition, our doctors may recommend follow-up visits to ensure your health is continuously monitored and managed effectively. We will work with you to establish a personalized healthcare plan that meets your specific needs.
