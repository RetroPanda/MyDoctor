About Us

My Doctor is a revolutionary healthcare service that brings medical consultations to your mobile or laptop device anywhere in South Africa or right to your doorstep in Cape Town. We understand that visiting a doctor's office can sometimes be inconvenient, especially for those with limited mobility or lack of transportation. With our virtual consults and home visit services, you can receive personalized medical care from the comfort and convenience of your own home.

Our Mission: At My Doctor, our mission is to provide accessible, convenient, and compassionate healthcare services to all South Africans. We strive to bridge the gap between patients and doctors, ensuring that everyone has the opportunity to receive highquality medical care, regardless of their circumstances.

Services

Virtual Consultations:

Our virtual consultations offer a convenient and efficient way to access medical advice from the comfort of your own home or office. Through secure video conferencing technology, you can discuss your symptoms, receive a diagnosis, and even get prescriptions, all without leaving your location. Our qualified doctors are available during extended hours, ensuring that you can access medical care when you need it most.

Call-Out Service:

We understand that there are instances where a physical examination is necessary to accurately diagnose and treat certain conditions. That's why we offer a call-out service bringing medical expertise right to your doorstep. Whether you have mobility issues, are bedridden, or simply prefer them. My Doctor Family Practice provides Call-Out visits to anywhere in Cape town, be it your home, office, AirBnB or Hotel.

We provide services to:

  1. Working professionals
  2. Stay-at-home parents
  3. The elderly or chronic illness sufferers who are too ill to be transported
  4. Local and international travellers
  5. LGBTQ individuals who would like to avoid discrimination or distress visiting a GP practice
  6. Anyone who would like to stay clear of overcrowded waiting rooms

How it works

Booking a visit from My Doctor Family Practice is a simple and hassle-free process.

Just follow these steps:
  1. Book your virtual consultation: Contact us through our website www.mydr.co.za to book an available timeslot for your appointment.
  2. Appointment Confirmation: Once we receive your request, we will confirm the date and time of your virtual consultation through SMS or email to the contact details you’ve provided. We strive to offer flexible scheduling options to accommodate your needs.
  3. Call-Out-Service: Select our call-out service from the list and one of our doctors will confirm via a telephonic call. Upon receiving payment, your booking will be arranged within 24 hours. On the scheduled day, one of our caring doctors will arrive at your location equipped with the necessary medical equipment and supplies. Our doctors take the time to listen to your concerns, perform necessary examinations, and provide appropriate treatment or guidance.
  4. Follow-up: Depending on your medical condition, our doctors may recommend follow-up visits to ensure your health is continuously monitored and managed effectively. We will work with you to establish a personalized healthcare plan that meets your specific needs.

We are committed to providing the highest standard of care to our patients. With our convenient virtual consulting and home visit service, you can take control of your health without stepping out of your front door. Experience healthcare in a whole new way with My Doctor!
