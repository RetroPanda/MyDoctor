Frequently Asked Questions

What services does My Doctor Family Practice offer?

We provide comprehensive primary healthcare services, including:

  • General medical consultations
  • Acute and chronic illness management
  • Women’s health services
  • Men’s health services
  • Mental health support
  • Gender-affirming care
  • Medical certificates and reports
  • Preventative care and health screenings
Services are available virtually, in person, and via home visits (where applicable).


Do you accept walk-in patients?

Yes, walk-ins are accepted.
Please note that patients with booked appointments are prioritised, so walk-in patients may experience a short wait depending on availability.


How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by:

  • Clicking the “Book Now” button on our website
  • Contacting us via WhatsApp or email, and our team will assist you

How do I access the video call?

You will be sent a confirmation SMS AND EMAIL to the contact details you’ve provided containing the link to access your consultation. At the time selected for your consultation, click the link and make sure your video and mic on your device has been switched on.

Is my consultation confidential?

Absolutely. All consultations are conducted in line with HPCSA ethical guidelines and POPIA regulations. Your personal and medical information is kept strictly confidential.


Can I get a medical certificate or medical report?

Yes. Medical certificates and reports are issued after a proper medical consultation and when clinically appropriate.

Do you provide gender-affirming care?

Yes. We provide inclusive, respectful, and patient-centred gender-affirming care for all individuals. Consultations can be virtual or in person.


What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring:

  • Your ID or passport
  • A list of current medications
  • Any previous medical records or test results (if available)


What if I need to cancel or reschedule my appointment?

We kindly ask that you notify us as early as possible if you need to cancel or reschedule, so we can offer the slot to another patient.


Who can I contact if I have further questions?

You are welcome to contact us via WhatsApp, email, or through the website contact form. Our team will be happy to assist you.
