  2. How do I access the video call?

    You will be sent a confirmation SMS to the number you’ve provided containing the link to access your consultation. At the time selected for you consultation, click the link and make sure your video and mic on your device has been switched on.

  3. How do I arrange for a Call-Out-Service?

    Select the call-out service and choose your date and timeslot. Upon receiving payment, your booking will be arranged within 24 hours.
